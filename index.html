Questions

Good to know.

What is Lu Apps?+
Lu Apps is a small American software studio — Lu Apps LLC, a United States company — building a growing family of privacy-first apps for Apple platforms. The name says it: “Lu” is short for Luxury. Every app is made with the same care and the same love.
Which apps are available now?+
Our flagship LuNote and LuSnake are live on the App Store today. LuHorary and LuTask are next, pending review, and the rest of the ecosystem is in active development. Each app on this page shows its current status.
How does Lu Apps handle my privacy?+
Privacy is a core principle, not a marketing line. We favor on-device processing and end-to-end encryption wherever possible, we never sell your data, and each app ships with a clear, readable privacy policy.
Which devices are supported?+
Our apps are built natively for iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Apple Watch, with deep integrations like Widgets, Shortcuts, and CarPlay where they make sense. Availability varies per app.
