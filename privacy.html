Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Overview

Lu Apps LLC (“Lu Apps”, “we”, “us”) operates this website, luapps.app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we handle when you visit the site and how we use it. We are privacy-first by design and collect as little as possible.

Each of our apps has its own privacy policy describing how that specific app handles data. This policy covers the website only.

Information we handle

Website analytics. We use Cloudflare Web Analytics, a privacy-first analytics service that does not use cookies and does not collect or store any personal data, IP addresses, or device fingerprints. It gives us only aggregate, anonymous statistics such as page views and referrers.

Contact form. If you send us a message through the contact form, we receive the name, email address, and message you choose to provide. We use this solely to read and reply to your message. Submissions are delivered to our support inbox through a form-processing provider on our behalf.

Cookies

This website does not use tracking or advertising cookies.

How we use information

We use the limited information above only to operate and improve the website and to respond to messages you send us. We do not use it for advertising.

Sharing

We do not sell or rent your information. We rely on a small number of service providers who process data on our behalf to run the site — for example our website host (GitHub Pages), our privacy-first analytics provider (Cloudflare), and our contact-form processor. These providers may only use the data to provide their service to us.

Data retention

Messages you send through the contact form are kept only as long as needed to assist you and maintain a record of our correspondence. You may ask us to delete them at any time.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information you have sent us. Just contact us through the form on the website and we will help.

Children

This website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Our apps

Individual Lu Apps products (such as LuNote and LuSnake) each have their own privacy policy that describes the data practices of that app. Please refer to the app's listing or in-app settings for details.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the website after changes means you accept the updated policy.
